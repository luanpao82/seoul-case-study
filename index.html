Final Research Project

Choose one format. Both require a comparative analysis of an SMG policy or management issue with U.S. practices.

Option A

Written Report

15–20 pages, double spaced. Literature review with 15–20 scholarly sources, methodology, findings, and policy implications. APA 7th edition.

Option B

Video Report

15–20 minute unlisted YouTube video. Must include field footage from Seoul.

  1. Introduction (2–3 min)
  2. Literature Review (3–5 min)
  3. Seoul Case Analysis (5–7 min)
  4. U.S. Comparison (3–4 min)
  5. Conclusion (1–2 min)

Submit with a 2–3 page supplementary document (APA references, 10+ sources, 250-word abstract).

Course Policies

Academic Honesty ▾
All work must be original. This course uses Turnitin — similarity scores should remain within 10%. Plagiarism includes unattributed copying, inadequate paraphrasing, and resubmitting work from other courses. Violations are reported to the Office of Student Conduct and may result in course failure.
Accessibility ▾
Students requiring accommodations must register with Student Accessibility Services (Ferrell Commons 7F, Room 185, 407-823-2371) before contacting the professor. This syllabus is available in alternate formats upon request.
Communication ▾
Official communication is through Webcourses email. Include a descriptive subject line. Expect responses within 24–48 hours on weekdays. Weekend and evening responses are not guaranteed.
